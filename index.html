Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes, every quote is completely free with no obligation to book. Message me the details of the job and I'll get back to you with a straight, honest price.

What areas do you cover?

I cover the North East and surrounding areas. Get in touch with your location and I'll confirm straight away if it's within reach.

Are you insured?

Yes, I'm fully insured for all repairs, maintenance and improvement work carried out.

How quickly can you start a job?

It depends on the size of the job, but I aim to reply to every enquiry the same day. Emergency call-outs are prioritised and I'll always tell you honestly if I can't get to something quickly.

Do you do stair renovations on older properties?

Yes, staircase makeovers, including stripping out old carpet runners and repainting treads, risers and handrails, are one of the jobs I get asked for most.

How do I get in touch?

The fastest way is to message me on Facebook or WhatsApp. You can also call or fill out the quote form on this site.
